About Us

Welcome to East & West Restaurant

Spice of India is where North Indian culinary traditions come to life in the heart of the UAE. We're passionate about crafting exceptional dishes that blend age-old recipes with modern flair.

Our passion for the art of blending spices and herbs with the freshest ingredients has made us a culinary destination in the UAE. From signature North Indian vegetarian dishes to a commitment to taste and health, we invite you to savor the magic of our cuisine, where tradition and innovation meet to create a truly memorable dining experience.
